Welcome to Agriculture

Agriculture is the practice of farming, including growing crops and raising animals. It provides food, raw materials, and employment to millions of people around the world.

Importance of Agriculture

  • Provides food for the population
  • Supports rural economy
  • Supplies raw materials to industries
  • Plays a key role in national development

Types of Agriculture

Crop Farming: Growing plants such as wheat, rice, maize, fruits, and vegetables for food and commercial use.

Livestock Farming: Rearing animals like cows, goats, sheep, and poultry for milk, meat, and eggs.

Modern Agriculture

Modern agriculture uses technology such as tractors, irrigation systems, fertilizers, and improved seeds to increase productivity and reduce labor.
